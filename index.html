Welcome to OmasKnitAndNibble – Your Online Store for Homemade Products

At OmasKnitAndNibble, we bring the wonderful, homemade products from grandmas and grandpas straight to your home. Our elderly community members often have valuable talents and a passion for baking and crocheting. We aim to provide them with a platform to share their creations while earning a small income.

Who We Are

We are three dedicated students who launched this project as part of our Web Engineering course. Our goal is to bridge generations and appreciate and support the craftsmanship of the older generation.

What We Offer

  • Homemade Treats: Discover delicious, homemade cakes, cookies, and breads, made with lots of love and traditional recipes.
  • Handmade Crochet Art: Browse a variety of handcrafted crochet items, from warm blankets to fashionable accessories to adorable stuffed animals.

Why OmasKnitAndNibble?

  • Quality and Authenticity: Each product is made with the utmost care and love by our talented grandmas and grandpas.
  • Support the Elderly Community: With each purchase, you help improve the living standards of our elderly community members and appreciate their talents.
  • Unique and Personal Gifts: Our products are perfect as personal gifts that truly come from the heart.
